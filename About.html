About the Artist

Abigail Platt is a Utah based artist originally from Boise, Idaho. She is currently pursuing a BA in Art at Brigham Young University, where she is expected to graduate in 2027. Growing up in nature-rich environments shaped her creative practice and continues to inspire her exploration of fluidity, growth, and environmental themes in her work.

As a multidisciplinary visual artist, Abigail works primarily in painting and sculpture. Her paintings are usually made in acrylic paints, while her sculptural works range in medium from found-objects to metal or wood. Occasionally, she branches out to other disciplines like print-making or ceramics. All of her pieces invite deeper conceptual thinking and connectedness between the natural world and the lives of her viewers.
